# Contributing

Contributions to this repository are welcome.

All contributions are made under the following licences:

- Code: Apache 2.0
- Text contributions in Markdown files: CC-BY-4.0

## Contributor License Agreement

Before your contribution can be merged, you must sign the Contributor License Agreement (CLA). When you open a pull request, the CLA Assistant bot will check whether you have signed and, if not, will guide you through it. Your pull request cannot be merged until the CLA check passes.

The CLA confirms that:

- You are the author of the contribution, or you otherwise have the right to submit it.
- Your contribution is your original work, or you have clearly identified any third-party material and confirmed it is compatible with the licences above.
- You grant your contribution under the applicable licence above (Apache 2.0 for code, CC-BY-4.0 for Markdown text).

If you are contributing on behalf of a company or other organization, make sure you are authorized to do so. Entities may need to sign a separate corporate CLA covering contributions made by their employees. The CLA Assistant bot will indicate which agreement applies.

## AI Contributions

Contributions created with the help of AI tools are welcome. The CLA applies in full: you remain responsible for ensuring you have the right to submit the contribution and that it does not include material under incompatible licences. You should also have reviewed the contribution, understand what it does, and verified that it works as intended.
